Our Sweet Menu (Services)
At Venus Ice Cream Parlour, we don't just serve dessert—we serve happiness in a bowl, cone, or glass. Here is a look at what we're scooping up fresh daily:

🍦 Premium Ice Creams
What’s Included: A massive rotating selection of rich, creamy ice creams, ranging from timeless classics like Vanilla and Belgian Chocolate to unique, handcrafted house specials. Available in crispy waffle cones or cups.

The Benefit: Pure, unadulterated bliss. Made with the freshest ingredients, our ice cream hits the spot every single time without breaking the bank.

What’s Included: Decadent layers of premium ice cream, loaded with gooey syrups, fresh nuts, whipped cream, and a cherry on top.

What’s Included: Thick, velvety shakes blended to absolute perfection using our premium ice cream and fresh milk, topped with fun garnishes.

The Benefit: On-the-go happiness. It’s the perfect refreshing treat to beat the Shiroor heat and keep you moving.

What’s Included: Warm, golden, crispy-on-the-outside waffles made fresh to order. Customized with your choice of ice cream scoops, sauces, and toppings.

The Benefit: The ultimate contrast of warm, crunchy waffle and cold, melting ice cream that creates a party in your mouth.

🍓 Limited-Edition Seasonal Desserts
What’s Included: Special creations featuring local, seasonal fruits like real mangoes, fresh berries, and traditional regional favorites.

The Benefit: You get to experience the best flavors of the season at their absolute peak of freshness.
